ABOUT THE BUSINESS

Grease & Shine All Purpose Degreaser is a trusted cleaning solution based in San Pablo City, Laguna. It effectively removes grease, oil, and dirt while helping prevent corrosion and rust on bicycles, motorcycles, vehicles, kitchens, and machinery. We serve retail and wholesale clients including bike shops, motor shops, and auto service centers.

MISSION

To provide powerful, high-quality, and affordable degreasers across Laguna while becoming the most trusted cleaning solution brand.

VISION

To become the leading and most reliable degreaser brand in San Pablo City and Laguna, recognized for quality, affordability, and continuous innovation.
